About

We built this as an entry to the second Riot Games API Challenge!

We decided to investigate how people used items differently after the MASSIVE OVERHAUL OF ALL THINGS AP of Patch 5.14.

But we weren't only interested in AP items. Mages get all the attention. What about the tanks? What about the supports? What about the...junglers?!?! *gasp* What kind of ripples did 5.14 thrust upon the sea of ranked play?

We'll show you. Or come explore. See the ripples!
